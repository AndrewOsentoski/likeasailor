Export iTunes Playlist

Open iTunes and navigate to your playlist, making sure that it is selected on the left hand pane.

Press the alt key on your keyboard, and select File from the top toolbar.

Select Library > Export Playlist... from the menu, then save the exported file somewhere you'll be able to find it as a .txt file.

Now, just hit the Choose File button in your browser, browse to your file, press Open, and hit Submit!
